About Us

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Thiruvananthapuram. We are at the forefront of providing exceptional education, research, and innovation in the field of computer science and technology.

Overview

Founded in 2001, the Department of Computer Science at the LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Thiruvananthapuram, has been a beacon of innovation and excellence. With a commitment to empowering the next generation of women in technology, we provide an educational experience that combines rigorous academic study and the excitement of discovery.

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure boasts advanced computing laboratories, research facilities, and collaborative workspaces. Students have access to the latest software and hardware technologies, ensuring they remain on the cutting edge of the field.
