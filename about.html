About the Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Thiruvananthapuram. We are at the forefront of providing exceptional education, research, and innovation in the field of computer science and technology.

A journey over the years

Our Commitment to Excellence

Since our inception in 2001, we have been committed to excellence in education and research. We take pride in being a driving force behind the rise of women in technology, and we continually strive to set new standards of achievement.

At the heart of our department is a commitment to academic excellence. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to combine the fundamentals of computer science with the latest industry trends. We foster critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a deep understanding of technology.

What We Do

Empowering Women: We believe in the power of diversity and are dedicated to empowering women to succeed in the tech industry.
World-Class Faculty: Our experienced faculty members are passionate about teaching and bring real-world experience to the classroom.
Cutting-Edge Research: We are at the forefront of computer science research, pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
Industry Connections: Our strong ties with industry leaders provide students with valuable networking opportunities and real-world exposure.
Inclusive Community: We foster a supportive and inclusive community where every student is encouraged to thrive.


Mission

M

To impart quality technical education by providing a conductive learning and research ambience for moulding ethically responsible engineers, academicians and researchers catering to the needs of industry and society.


Vision

V

To be a renowned academic and research centre in Computer Science and allied domains.





History

H

Formed in 2001, we have over 3500 students around the globe making a change in the world.
